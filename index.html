WHO WE ARE

Real hardware. Real code. Real understanding.

Siddham Robotics is a hands-on STEM education initiative that believes learning happens best when you build, break, and rebuild things with your own hands. We don't teach from slides. We hand students real hardware, real electrodes, and real code — and let them experience science the way it was always meant to be felt.

From a single 2-hour seminar to a structured 6-month course — we design every program around the student's age, goal, and available time. We work online and travel anywhere in India where curiosity is celebrated.

"Yeh sirf ek course nahi hai — yeh ek experience hai."

🧑‍💻
Sanket Jain
Founder & Lead Educator

THE PERSON BEHIND IT

Not just a teacher.
A fellow curious mind.

I started Siddham Robotics because I believe every child deserves to experience the magic of building something real. Not reading about circuits — building them. Not hearing about brain signals — seeing their own on a screen.

I've personally taught 1,000+ students across workshops, structured courses, and live sessions. Every program I design comes from one simple question: what would have made me fall in love with this earlier?

I teach Electronics, Arduino, Robotics, AI, Neuroscience, Python, IoT, Drones, and 3D Printing. And I bring this to schools, colleges, and anyone who's curious — online or in person, wherever you are in India.

Robotics Systems

Machine Learning & AI

Neuroscience & Brain Signals

Python Programming

3D Printing & Design
Design it digitally. Print it physically. Hold your idea in your hands.

Drone Technology
How drones fly. The electronics behind UAVs. Aerial systems from first principles.
